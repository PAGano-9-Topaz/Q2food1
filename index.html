Kare-Kare

Step 1: Blanch Meat chunks and tripe

Ingredients needed: Chuck roast and tripe

Step 2: remove Meat and tripe, then clean them

Step 3: Sauté garlic and shallots

Ingredients: garlic and shallots

Step 4: Add meat and tripe, then cover - 7min

Step 5: Add water to cover meat, then add fish sauce and pepper

Ingredients: fish sauce and pepper

Step 6: strain annatto seeds color from boiling water

Step 7: when meat is tender - add banana and peanut butter

ingredients: banana blossoms and peanut butter

Step 8: add the vegetables

Ingredients: bok choy and egg plant

Step 9: add annatto seeds color and enough water for consistency

Step 10: Continue cooking for the vegetables. then serve.

Ingredients

1 Whole garlic

3 shallots

1 kilo large chunked chuck roast

½ kilo tripe

2 tbsp fish sauce

ground black pepper

2 banana blossoms

7 pc chinese long beans

2 Japanese egg plant - sliced

1 bunch bok choy

1 cup peanut butter

2 tbsp annatto seeds - in 1 cup boiling water
